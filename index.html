Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about booking your luxury getaway.

How far in advance should I book my luxury tour?

We recommend booking at least 3 to 6 months in advance for peak seasons and exclusive properties. However, we also specialize in curated last-minute luxury escapes.

Are flights included in the tour packages?

Many of our premium packages include international business or first-class flights. Package details clearly specify what is included, and we can customize any itinerary to add flight arrangements.

What is your cancellation policy?

We offer flexible cancellation terms. Most bookings can be canceled up to 45 days before departure for a full refund, minus a small administrative fee. Travel insurance is highly recommended.

Do you provide visa assistance?

Yes, our dedicated visa support team provides comprehensive guidance, document checking, and application submission services for all destinations we operate in.
